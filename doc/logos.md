## Notes

- **Resend / tRPC** — minimal wordmarks, no icon at all
- **Supabase / Temporal** — single abstract glyph that works as a favicon
- **Sentry** — geometric shape (eye/hexagon hybrid) that reads at 16px
- **Prisma** — outlined triangle, instantly recognizable at any size
- **Stripe** — gradient `S` that encodes the brand in one letter

Temporal is the closest analog to bpmnkit — also a workflow/process SDK, uses simple geometric overlapping shapes suggesting flow.
